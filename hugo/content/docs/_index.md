Welcome to the Zero Trust Analytics documentation. Here you'll find everything you need to integrate privacy-first analytics into your website or application.

## What is Zero Trust Analytics?

Zero Trust Analytics is a privacy-first website analytics platform that gives you actionable insights without compromising your visitors' privacy. We don't use cookies, we don't store IP addresses, and we don't track users across the web.

## Key Features

- **No cookies** - No consent banners needed
- **No IP logging** - IPs are hashed with a daily rotating salt
- **GDPR/CCPA compliant** - By design, not by checkbox
- **Lightweight** - Under 3KB script, won't slow your site
- **Real-time analytics** - See who's on your site right now
- **Custom events** - Track clicks, forms, and custom actions
- **API access** - Build your own dashboards and integrations
