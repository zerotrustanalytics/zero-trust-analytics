Privacy Policy

Zero Trust Analytics is committed to protecting your privacy and developing technology that gives you the most powerful and safe online experience. This Statement of Privacy applies to Zero Trust Analytics site and governs data collection and usage. By using Zero Trust Analytics site, you consent to the data practices described in this statement.

Collection of your Personal Information

Zero Trust Analytics collects personally identifiable information, such as your email address, name, home or work address or telephone number. Zero Trust Analytics also collects anonymous demographic information, which is not unique to you, such as your ZIP code, age, gender, preferences, interests and favorites.

There is also information about your computer hardware and software that is automatically collected by Zero Trust Analytics . This information can include: your IP address, browser type, domain names, access times and referring website addresses. This information is used by Zero Trust Analytics for the operation of the service, to maintain quality of the service, and to provide general statistics regarding use of Zero Trust Analytics site.

Please keep in mind that if you directly disclose personally identifiable information or personally sensitive data through Zero Trust Analytics public message boards, this information may be collected and used by others. Note: Zero Trust Analytics does not read any of your private online communications.

Zero Trust Analytics encourages you to review the privacy statements of Web sites you choose to link to from Zero Trust Analytics so that you can understand how those Web sites collect, use and share your information. Zero Trust Analytics is not responsible for the privacy statements or other content on Web sites outside of Zero Trust Analytics and Zero Trust Analytics family of Web sites.

Use of your Personal Information

Zero Trust Analytics collects and uses your personal information to operate Zero Trust Analytics Web site and deliver the services you have requested. Zero Trust Analytics also uses your personally identifiable information to inform you of other products or services available from Zero Trust Analytics and its affiliates. Zero Trust Analytics may also contact you via surveys to conduct research about your opinion of current services or of potential new services that may be offered.

Zero Trust Analytics does not sell, rent or lease its customer lists to third parties. Zero Trust Analytics may, from time to time, contact you on behalf of external business partners about a particular offering that may be of interest to you. In those cases, your unique personally identifiable information (e-mail, name, address, telephone number) is not transferred to the third party. In addition, Zero Trust Analytics may share data with trusted partners to help us perform statistical analysis, send you email or postal mail, provide customer support, or arrange for deliveries. All such third parties are prohibited from using your personal information except to provide these services to Zero Trust Analytics , and they are required to maintain the confidentiality of your information.

Zero Trust Analytics does not use or disclose sensitive personal information, such as race, religion, or political affiliations, without your explicit consent.

Zero Trust Analytics keeps track of the Web sites and pages our customers visit within Zero Trust Analytics , in order to determine what Zero Trust Analytics services are the most popular. This data is used to deliver customized content and advertising within Zero Trust Analytics to customers whose behavior indicates that they are interested in a particular subject area.

Zero Trust Analytics Web sites will disclose your personal information, without notice, only if required to do so by law or in the good faith belief that such action is necessary to: (a) conform to the edicts of the law or comply with legal process served on Zero Trust Analytics or the site; (b) protect and defend the rights or property of Zero Trust Analytics ; and, (c) act under exigent circumstances to protect the personal safety of users of Zero Trust Analytics , or the public.

Use of Cookies

Zero Trust Analytics Web site use "cookies" to help you personalize your online experience. A cookie is a text file that is placed on your hard disk by a Web page server. Cookies cannot be used to run programs or deliver viruses to your computer. Cookies are uniquely assigned to you, and can only be read by a web server in the domain that issued the cookie to you.

One of the primary purposes of cookies is to provide a convenience feature to save you time. The purpose of a cookie is to tell the Web server that you have returned to a specific page. For example, if you personalize Zero Trust Analytics pages, or register with Zero Trust Analytics site or services, a cookie helps Zero Trust Analytics to recall your specific information on subsequent visits. This simplifies the process of recording your personal information, such as billing addresses, shipping addresses, and so on. When you return to the same Zero Trust Analytics Web site, the information you previously provided can be retrieved, so you can easily use Zero Trust Analytics features that you customized.

You have the ability to accept or decline cookies. Most Web browsers automatically accept cookies, but you can usually modify your browser setting to decline cookies if you prefer. If you choose to decline cookies, you may not be able to fully experience the interactive features of Zero Trust Analytics services or Web sites you visit.

Security of your Personal Information

Zero Trust Analytics secures your personal information from unauthorized access, use or disclosure. Zero Trust Analytics secures the personally identifiable information you provide on computer servers in a controlled, secure environment, protected from unauthorized access, use or disclosure. When personal information (such as a credit card number) is transmitted to other Web sites, it is protected through the use of encryption, such as the Secure Socket Layer (SSL) protocol.

Changes to this Statement

Zero Trust Analytics will occasionally update this Statement of Privacy to reflect company and customer feedback. Zero Trust Analytics encourages you to periodically review this Statement to be informed of how Zero Trust Analytics is protecting your information.
