Privacy First

What We DON'T Collect

No Cookies

We never set cookies on your visitors' browsers. Zero cookie banners needed.

No IP Addresses

We never store raw IP addresses. Only one-way hashed identifiers with daily rotation.

No Fingerprinting

No canvas fingerprinting, font detection, or any device fingerprinting techniques.

No Personal Data

No names, emails, or any personally identifiable information stored.

No Cross-Site Tracking

Visitors are never tracked across different websites. Each site is isolated.

No Data Selling

Your analytics data is yours alone. We never sell or share it with third parties.
